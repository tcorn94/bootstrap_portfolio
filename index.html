About Me

I was born in South Florida, but I have lived in a few different places throughout my life. I lived in Vancouver, WA for a year, and Nerima, a prefecture in Tokyo, Japan. I have studied a lot of things, but my major in college was Spanish literature and linguistics. I'm *ALMOST* a certified veterinary assistant, and I'm *ALMOST* a state certified teacher. I've been a tutor, a teacher, and for the last two years I've been delivering pizzas. My main interest is traveling, and I've visited more than 15 countries so far. There is still so much I'd love to see. From a coding perspective I'm interested in creating an audio sharing platform for a niche pop culture community, but the main idea is to increase accessibility to certain fan created works for the visually impaired.
